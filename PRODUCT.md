# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
CSPC ACCESS reviewees. Students and professionals preparing for the Civil Service Examination (CSE-PPT). Academic, high-stakes, testing environment.

## Product Purpose
Adaptive review platform for CSE-PPT. Provides diagnostic testing, personalized study plans, and domain-level analytics to ensure reviewees reach the 80% passing threshold.

## Brand Personality
Institutional, Authoritative, High-Contrast, Professional, Trustworthy.

## Anti-references
- SaaS-generic glassmorphism (blurs, noisy gradients).
- "Slop" (decorative elements with no function).
- Bento grids or overly playful "startup" aesthetics.
- Faint borders or low-contrast text.

## Design Principles
- **Visual Authority**: Use rigid geometry and bold weights to signal official institutional status.
- **High Information Density, Zero Noise**: Every element serves the educational goal. No decorative "fluff."
- **High-Contrast Clarity**: WCAG AA/AAA compliance as a design feature, not an afterthought.
- **Physical Depth**: Use layered semantic shadows and solid surfaces instead of translucency.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA minimum, AAA target for color contrast.
- Reduced motion support for critical transitions.
- Screen reader friendly semantic HTML.
